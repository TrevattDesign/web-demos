Therapeutic approaches

Evidence-based therapy,
tailored to you

01

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

A structured, evidence-based approach that helps you understand the links between your thoughts, feelings and behaviour, and change the patterns that are keeping you stuck. Widely recommended for anxiety, depression and low self-esteem.

02

Psychoanalytic and Psychodynamic Therapy

Exploring how past experiences and unconscious processes shape how you feel and behave today. This approach creates deeper self-understanding and more lasting change by working with the roots of your difficulties.

03

Humanistic Therapy

Centred on your natural capacity for growth, self-awareness and healing. Sessions are grounded in warmth, empathy and unconditional positive regard, helping you reconnect with who you are and what you need.

04

Integrative Approach

Drawing flexibly from multiple modalities, I create an individualised treatment plan based on your specific needs, goals and what the evidence shows works best for your difficulties. No two programmes are the same.
