About Phoebe

Trained in the body as well as the mind

Before I became a psychotherapist, I spent over 15 years working as a performer. That time taught me things about the body, about presence, and about what it takes to keep showing up that no textbook could have. It also gave me a particular understanding of what the creative life can ask of you, and what it can take.

I hold a Master's in Counselling and Psychotherapy from the University of Roehampton and am a registered member of the BACP. I trained in somatic trauma therapy with world-renowned specialist Babette Rothschild, and I bring that body-aware, trauma-informed approach to all of my work.

I work with individuals in private practice in Surbiton and London, and I am a sessional therapist at Nightingale Hospital. I have worked in NHS settings, with the low-cost service Headstrong, and with young creatives at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts. I offer a GSRD-affirmative space and have extensive experience working with members of the LGBTQIA+ community.

I work with millennials and Gen Z clients in particular, including creatives and freelancers who know the particular pressures of an uncertain, portfolio-based working life.

How I work

Grounded in the body, led by you

I draw from several therapeutic traditions and adapt to what each person actually needs. The common thread is that we pay attention to the whole of you, not just the story your mind is telling.

01

Somatic and body-aware

Trauma lives in the nervous system, not just in memory. Drawing on my training with Babette Rothschild, I work with what the body holds as well as what the mind knows.

02

Person-centred at the core

You are the expert on your own experience. My job is to offer a consistently safe, non-judgmental space in which you can start to hear yourself more clearly.

03

Psychodynamic depth

Old patterns often have old roots. Where it's useful, we look at how early relationships and experiences continue to shape how you move through the world today.

04

Creative and imaginal

I use imagery, metaphor, and embodied awareness to open up what words alone can't always reach. Particularly useful for creative clients, though never obligatory.

05

GSRD-affirmative

I offer an explicitly affirming space for LGBTQIA+ clients, people exploring gender and sexuality, and those whose identities sit outside mainstream frameworks.

06

Flexible in length

I offer short-term focused work, longer open-ended therapy, and single-session therapy. We'll work out together what fits your life and your goals.
