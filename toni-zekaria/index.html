About Toni

A counsellor shaped by a life among people

Before I became a counsellor, I spent several years managing a luxury boutique hotel, and then built and ran my own bakery and cafe. Both careers put me in front of people every day, asking variations of the same question: what do you need, and how can I help? Those years gave me an ease with people, and a real interest in them, that no professional training could have provided on its own.

I am a BACP Registered Counsellor working with adults of all ages. I take an integrative approach, drawing from a range of evidence-based methods and adapting to each individual rather than applying a fixed model. I work with individuals and with couples.

Sessions are available in person at an NHS GP surgery in Hendon NW4, and online or by phone from anywhere in the UK. I also offer sessions in French. My work is fully insured and professionally supervised in line with BACP guidelines.

Integrative counselling means I draw from a range of therapeutic approaches rather than following a single fixed method. This might include person-centred work, psychodynamic thinking, CBT-informed techniques, and others. The approach is tailored to you and what you bring, rather than fitting you to a pre-set programme.

The first session is a chance to talk about what has brought you to counselling and what you are hoping to get from it. There is no pressure to have everything clear or to cover your whole history. We take it at whatever pace feels right, and it is also an opportunity for you to get a feel for how I work and whether it seems like a good fit.
